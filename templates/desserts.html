Desserts Recipes

Gulaab Jamun

Servings: 10

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup khoya (milk solids)
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 4-5 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp rose water
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix khoya, flour, and baking soda; knead.
  2. Add milk; form a smooth dough.
  3. Shape into small balls.
  4. Heat oil; fry balls until golden brown.
  5. Boil sugar, water, and cardamom for syrup.
  6. Simmer syrup until slightly thickened.
  7. Add rose water to syrup.
  8. Soak fried balls in syrup for 1-2 hours.

Serve: Warm or chilled.

Ghee Mysore Pak

Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Grease a plate with ghee; set aside.
  2. Mix besan and 2 tbsp ghee in a bowl.
  3. Heat sugar and water until it reaches one-string consistency.
  4. Slowly add the besan mixture to the sugar syrup, stirring constantly.
  5. Gradually add the remaining ghee, stirring continuously.
  6. Cook until the mixture starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  7. Pour the mixture onto the greased plate.
  8. Allow it to cool slightly, then cut into pieces.

Serve: Once cooled, serve as a sweet snack.

Tiramisu

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups brewed espresso, cooled
  • 2 tbsp rum
  • 2 packages ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick.
  2. Add mascarpone; mix until smooth.
  3. Whip cream to stiff peaks; fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Combine espresso and rum.
  5. Dip ladyfingers in espresso mixture; layer in dish.
  6. Spread mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers.
  7. Repeat layers, ending with mascarpone mixture.
  8. Dust with cocoa powder.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

Serve: Chilled.

Kaju Katli

Servings: 20 pieces

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cashew nuts
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • Silver vark (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil water and sugar to make syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder to syrup.
  4. Stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  5. Add ghee and cardamom; mix well.
  6. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  7. Cool slightly and knead into dough.
  8. Roll out dough; apply silver vark.
  9. Cut into diamond shapes.

Serve: Store in an airtight container.

Rasmalai

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts
  • Few saffron strands
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk

Instructions:

  1. Curdle milk with lemon juice; strain paneer.
  2. Knead paneer; form small balls.
  3. Boil water with sugar; cook balls for 15 mins.
  4. Boil milk; add condensed milk, cardamom, saffron.
  5. Add balls to milk; chill for 2-3 hrs.
  6. Garnish with nuts; serve cold.

Serve: Cold dessert.

Kulfi

Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • Few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan.
  2. Simmer, stirring frequently until reduced to half.
  3. Add condensed milk and sugar; mix well.
  4. Add cardamom powder, nuts, and saffron; cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Cool the mixture to room temperature.
  6. Pour into molds; freeze for 6-8 hours.
  7. Unmold and serve chilled.

Serve: Plain or with falooda.

Paneer Butter Masala

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup onion
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ cup cream
  • Salt, coriander

Instructions:

  1. Cut and optionally fry paneer.
  2. Sauté onion in butter.
  3. Add ginger-garlic; cook.
  4. Add tomato puree; cook.
  5. Add spices; mix.
  6. Add paneer; cook.
  7. Add cream; simmer.
  8. Garnish with coriander.

Serve: Naan or rice.

Chole Bhature

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chole masala
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Oil, salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook chickpeas (20 mins).
  2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic.
  3. Add tomato puree; cook.
  4. Mix in masala and chickpeas.
  5. Knead flour and yogurt; rest.
  6. Roll and fry bhature.

Serve: Hot with Chole.

Masala Dosa

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup urad dal
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • 2 mashed potatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, dal, fenugreek 6-8 hrs.
  2. Grind; ferment overnight.
  3. Add salt; spread batter on skillet.
  4. Cook; add filling, fold.

Serve: With chutney.

Baby Corn Manchurian

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 250g baby corn
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • Mixed bell peppers
  • Salt, oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Cut baby corn.
  2. Mix flours, ginger-garlic, and salt.
  3. Dredge baby corn; deep fry until golden.
  4. Sauté peppers; add sauces.
  5. Combine with baby corn; toss.

Serve: Hot, with rice or noodles.

Veggie Cheese Pizza

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. Spread sauce on base; add cheese and veggies.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes; cool for 5 minutes.

Serve: Enjoy with extra toppings.

Bhel Puri

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1 cup sev
  • 1 cup onions, chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ cup boiled potatoes, cubed
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 2 tbsp green chutney
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 2 tbsp coriander, chopped
  • Salt, lemon juice to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine puffed rice, sev, onions, tomatoes, and potatoes.
  2. Add chutneys, mix in spices.
  3. Garnish with coriander; serve immediately.

Serve: With fresh coriander garnish.

Pink Sauce Pasta

Pour in heavy cream, stir well, and simmer for 2-3 minutes. Mix in grated Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce until coated. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot. French Fries

To make Peri Peri French Fries, cut potatoes into fries and soak in cold water for 30 minutes. Drain and pat dry. Fry in hot oil until golden and crispy. Mix fries with peri peri seasoning (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, oregano, and salt). Toss well to coat. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy your spicy, flavorful Peri Peri French Fries!
